Hooked on Strings

What we're all About

This is a club made by string-craft lovers, for string-craft lovers. We get together every month to work on knitting, crocheting, embriodery, cross-stich or any other string based project. We also have beginner lessons if you're looking to learn a new hobby. Our club is for all ages and skill levels so everyone is welcome to stop by if you're interested!

Monthly Meetings

We have scheduled meetings each month at Yarn Works. These meetings are for members to work on their projects and for people to stop by and see what we are about. Since we are a diverse group there are people of different skill levels and experiences so don't feel like you have to be an expert to attend! Every one is welcome and we love new members so don't hesitate to stop by or contact us if you're interested.

Lessons

As mentioned we do beginner lessons for knitting, crochet and embriodery. These lessons are not taught at every one of our meetings. If you're interested in a lesson we ask that you contact us to set one up. We can have lessons up to once a week depending on interest so call or email us to let us know.
